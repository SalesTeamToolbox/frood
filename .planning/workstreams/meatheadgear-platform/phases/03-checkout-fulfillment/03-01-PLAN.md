**Note on PurchaseRequest and color:** The `PurchaseRequest` sends `design_id`, `product_id`, and `size` but not an explicit `color` field. This is intentional — in Printful's data model, each `variant_id` uniquely encodes a size+color combination. The variant_id is resolved from `product_id + size` (and implicitly color) in `create_product_checkout()` via the `product_variants` table, where rows are keyed by `(product_id, size, color)`. No separate color field is needed in the checkout request.
